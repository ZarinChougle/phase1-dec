# Azure AZ-900 – Cloud Concepts (Day 1 Notes)

## What is Cloud Computing?
Delivery of computing services (servers, storage, networking, databases, software) over the internet.

## Benefits:
- High availability  
- Scalability  
- Elasticity  
- Reliability  
- Global reach  
- Cost efficiency (OpEx > CapEx)

## Cloud Models:
- IaaS – Virtual Machines  
- PaaS – App Services  
- SaaS – Office 365
